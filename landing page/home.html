Our Philosophy


Today, we are still fascinated by space and the idea of space travel. Space continues to represent a new frontier for mankind, fascinating and scaring us in equal measure. We continue to push the boundaries of our knowledge, awaiting the day when travel into space is as open and accessible as air travel.

This is a space you can use to talk to fellow budding astronomers and space enthusiasts. Go ahead and indulge in discussions on our Forums page. You can even look up astronomical equipment in the Store.

Space is bigger and greater than anything we can imagine. So make BITSPACE your space.

Happy Exploring!
